Yoga Poses


Poses Benefits
Mountain Pose (Tadasana) Stand tall with feet together, arms by your sides. Engage the thighs, lift the chest, and reach your arms overhead with palms facing each other . Warrrior I (Virabhadrasana I): Strengthens the legs, opens the hips, chest, and shoulders; improves balance and concentration; builds stamina and focus. Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II): Strengthens the legs and ankles; opens the hips, chest, and shoulders; improves stamina and concentration. Tree Pose (Vrikshasana): Improves balance and concentration; strengthens the thighs, calves, and ankles; stretches the groins and inner thighs. Child pose Relieves back and neck pain; stretches the hips, thighs, and ankles; promotes relaxation and stress relief.
